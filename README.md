# CamTrapAsia: a dataset of tropical forest vertebrate communities from 239 camera trapping studies

Dataset containing data from 239Â camera trap studies conducted in tropical Asia. A total of 278,260 independent records were compiled, from 371 distinct species, comprising 232 mammals, 132 birds, and 7 reptiles. The accumulated trapping effort was 876,606 trap nights, distributed among Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Bhutan, Thailand, Myanmar, Cambodia, Laos, Vietnam, Nepal and far-eastern India.
